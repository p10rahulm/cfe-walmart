Postdoctoral Researcher Positions at IISc
Multiple postdoctoral fellowship opportunities are available within the Algorithms group at IISc. The position comes with a competitive salary (Rs 1,30,000 per month) and appropriate travel support. The fellowship is a one-year full-time appointment, with the possibility of renewal for a second year. The postdoc is welcome to work in any of the active research areas at IISc. The applicants should have a strong background and a doctorate in a related field of Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or Electrical Engineering. We encourage applications from underrepresented minorities in STEM.

If you are interested in Algorithms and Theory (e.g., approximation algorithms, algorithmic game theory, computational geometry, online and dynamic algorithms, spectral algorithms, data structures, and theoretical machine learning), and have published in top-tier conferences/journals, please apply.

Internships (Predocs/Interns/Research Associates) at IISc
Multiple internship/predoc/RA positions are available within the Algorithms group at IISc. The positions come with a salary of Rs 40,000 per month. The positions are open to bright and motivated candidates pursuing/completed Bachelor’s (B.E./B.Tech/B.Stat/B.Math/B.Sc or equivalent) or Master’s (M.E./M.Tech/M.Stat/M.Sc or equivalent) in CS/ECE/EE/Maths or equivalent programs. The internship program is a full-time research commitment for students pursuing the above-mentioned degree programs for at least two months.

The predoc program is a full-time research position for people who have already completed their degree requirements and is a longer commitment (minimum six months, up to two years). If you are interested in Algorithms and Theory and have done advanced theory courses, please apply.

For any other queries, please send us an email at theoryapp@iisc.ac.in and we'll be happy to connect with you.
