Sayak is a second-year M.Tech student in AI at the Future Computing Systems (FIST) Lab, department of Computer Science and Automation at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, working with Prof. Sumit Kumar Mandal. His research investigates post-Von Neumann architectures, with a focus on designing ReRAM-based Compute-In-Memory (CIM) accelerators for Large Foundational Models. He is also interested in Efficient LLM Inference, Hardware-Software Co-design, and HPC.

Previously, he completed his M.Sc. in Computer Science at Ramakrishna Mission Vivekananda Educational and Research Institute and his B.Sc. at Ramakrishna Mission Residential College, Narendrapur.

Post-Von Neumann architectures, Efficient LLM Inference
