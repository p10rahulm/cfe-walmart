A Center for Algorithms, Edge Computing, and Learning
About the Center
WACE is a Walmart initiative aimed at conducting impactful and transformational research at the frontiers of Algorithms, Edge Computing, and Learning.
Specific thrust areas of WACE include:
  • Modern algorithms for optimization problems that arise in analytic, economic, and planning contexts.
  • Energy-efficient processing-in-edge for large scale machine learning.
  • Redesigning reinforcement learning methods for scalability and robustness.
WACE will focus on foundational research and, in the next five years, strive to be a leading research hub for the above-mentioned areas. Further, by way of conducting cutting-edge research, WACE aims to play a pivotal role in the development of preeminent researchers and strengthen India's global research presence.
WACE will actively engage in:
  • Conducting foundational research in algorithms, learning, and edge computing.
  • Supporting Postdocs, Predocs, and PhD students.
  • Research dissemination via national and international workshops.
  • International collaborations.
